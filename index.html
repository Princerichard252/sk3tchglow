Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your new skincare routine.

How often should I apply the serum?

expand_more

For best results, we recommend applying the serum twice daily—once in the morning after cleansing and once at night before your heavy moisturizer or night cream.

Is it suitable for sensitive skin?

expand_more

Absolutely! Our formula is dermatologically tested, hypoallergenic, and free from harsh parabens, artificial fragrances, and sulfates, making it incredibly safe for sensitive and acne-prone skin types.

How long does nationwide shipping take?

expand_more

Orders within Lagos usually arrive within 24 to 48 hours. For outside Lagos (Abuja, Port Harcourt, etc.), expect delivery within 3 to 5 business days via our trusted courier partners.

Can I wear makeup over the serum?

expand_more

Yes! Because the serum features a fast-absorption formula, it dries down beautifully without leaving a sticky residue, making it the perfect hydrating primer base for your daily makeup.
